When you invite someone to join OpenA.com, they become part of your network.

As you build your social network on our site we track that growth and reward you for helping OpenA.com grow.

My Network

Shows the total number of people that you have had a hand in introducing to OpenA.com. This number not only reflects people you have directly invited, but also people your friends have invited and so on. As your network grows in depth and size this number will adjust accordingly.

Total Retail Value

Shows the total retail value of all products listed in our marketplace for all members of your network.Whenever an item is sold on our marketplace it generates revenue to OpenA.com. We share a portion of this revenue back to our independent brand ambassadors.

Total Transection Value

Shows the total dollar amount of transactions that have occured within your network. As an independent brand ambassador you can be positioned to earn a portion of this value. To learn more about becoming a brand ambassador click here
